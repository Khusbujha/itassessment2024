About Us

Bookiepedia is a platform dedicated to book lovers and readers, who are looking for books to thrift. This platform was initiated by high school students in 2022 and since has been in the process of development. This wwbsite is created as a fussion between a bookstore and a smart phone.

We aim to provide a wide range of books, from fiction to non-fiction, and everything in between. Our goal is to create a community of readers who can share their love for books and discover new titles together.Through our platform, users can easily search and purchase books from the comfort of their own homes.
